About Dana

At an early age, Dana became fascinated with how people incorporate technology within ever-developing paradigms. She graduated UT Austin with a dual bachelors degree in a selective honors program, balancing her studies between graduate-level sciences and communication studies.

As she pursued her masters degree in the integration of global cultural medicine and modern science, the technological discrepancy between cultural healing services and modern medicine became evident to her.

Dana began to work with businesses that were struggling to incorporate technology into their procedures: first by using, then modifying, and finally writing applications to create smoother workflows and higher levels of accessibility for her clients. She is now ready to join a product team and build streamlined, clarified, and dependable applications.
